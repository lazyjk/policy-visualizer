## Fixes

- **Export: black background on PDF/PNG without transparent option** — `backgroundColor`
  was applied to the CSS-translated `.react-flow__viewport` element, offsetting the white
  fill rect and leaving transparent strips that JPEG encoded as black. Fix: always capture
  as transparent PNG, then composite onto a white canvas before encoding (PNG or JPEG).
- **Export: blurry output on medium/large diagrams** — `EXPORT_MAX_PIXELS = 16 MP` caused
  `pixelRatio` to be clamped to 1 for typical diagrams. Increased to 64 MP; typical
  diagrams now render at pixelRatio 3–4, large diagrams at 2.
- **Export: PDF file size** (PR #3) — switched PDF intermediate from PNG to JPEG (0.92
  quality), reducing typical exports from ~70 MB to ~1.8 MB.
- **Export: viewport-independent capture** (PR #3) — replaced `fitView()` + double-rAF
  race condition with direct `.react-flow__viewport` targeting at scale=1.
